HOME BIODATA PORTFOLIO EXPERIENCE CONTACT ME


My parents had always told me that it is extremely important to
receive a diploma and a bachelor's degree not only because of acquisition of new knowledge but
also because of the different experiences that people receive in four years at the university.
My experience so far at UiTM has been a roller coaster ride.
It has had its ups and downs similar to any other new experience to a person.
As a freshman it takes some adjusting to get used to the style,
especially when I have had the support from my parents for a long time.
I have had moments here that I have enjoyed, and other ones that I would rather not have to deal with.


It has been a few months since I last finished my diploma and pursuing my bachelor's degree,
but it definitely has been an experience I will need in the future and it is better to learn it now than later on down the road.
One of the biggest thing that was a big change was going away, and not living at home.
I have never been on an extended stay away from home, and I’m enjoying it to an extent.
However, due to the pandemic season all the university were closed and students were asked to have an online classes instead.
My online learning experience as a student is not so good.
I have to wake up early every day and I'm not a morning person.
In addition, online classes is confusing when the lecturer gives us work mostly since it's easier for me to do work
with instructions that the lecturer gives.
Being in a physical classroom makes me only focus on my work, but online learning makes it harder to focus
because I have to also focus on household stuff, and it throws me off.
I would not want to do online learning in the future because it is hard for me to concentrate on my work only.


Nevertheless, UiTM is a place that truly helps shaping one's personality.
UiTM is like another Godsend gift for me. It is one of the best universities in my entire vicinity.
We are truly cared, rightly educated and well socialized here. I myself feel proud of being a student here.
I do take care of what is being taught and what is to learn.
I respect my lecturers, my fellow beings, my university and everything that is associated with it.


My university experiences thus far have been amazing, despite the hard things and a very hectic life.
I have learned and experienced so many new things in such a short period of time, and it has gone by so fast.
I am still discovering all the opportunities that university offers me.
UiTM is a very welcoming place, and from the first day, I felt at home here.
I have made many new friends from many different backgrounds.
There are from Kedah, Perlis and many more and that has allowed me an insight into their cultures.


I am a good student, I study well.
The proudest action I have encountered,
would be making the Dean’s List as I ended my last semester at UiTM Kedah with a 3.70 GPA.
I do not think my parents have been more
proud of me than in the moment they found out how well I had done.
But I think I could do better in future and appreciate every positive sentiment.
Also, I am grateful of the genuine love and support that I have received from my family and close friends.
